I've been using Claude since its first public release in March 2023. Since then, I've watched Anthropic grow at a pace that's unmatched even in the AI industry. From $10 million in revenue in 2022 to $19 billion in annualized revenue in March 2026.

In this article, I've compiled the latest numbers, data and facts about Claude and Anthropic. From funding rounds and valuation to benchmarks and model development to enterprise adoption and safety research.

TL;DRKey Takeaways

- Anthropic is valued at $380 billion (February 2026), has received over $50 billion in funding and is approaching $19 billion in annualized revenue
- Claude Opus 4.5 was the first model to break the 80% mark on SWE-bench Verified. Claude Opus 4.6 holds the #1 position on the LMSYS High-Elo Bracket
- Anthropic employs over 2,000 people and is a pioneer in Constitutional AI and AI safety research

Note

Unlike OpenAI, Anthropic does not publish detailed user numbers. This article therefore focuses on verifiable data: funding, revenue, valuation, benchmarks, traffic data and technical specifications.

## 1\. Company Overview and Milestones

Anthropic was founded in 2021 by Dario and Daniela Amodei along with five other former OpenAI researchers. The reason for the split was concerns about AI safety and alignment at OpenAI.

Since then, the company has achieved remarkable growth:

| Date | Milestone | Details |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2021 | Founded | Dario & Daniela Amodei + 5 ex-OpenAI researchers |
| 2022 | Constitutional AI | Publication of the Constitutional AI research paper |
| March 2023 | Claude 1.0 | First public model with 9K token context window |
| July 2023 | Claude 2 | 100K token context window, significant performance improvement |
| March 2024 | Claude 3 Family | Haiku, Sonnet and Opus. Opus first #1 on LMSYS |
| June 2024 | Claude 3.5 Sonnet | Faster than Opus at lower cost |
| October 2024 | Computer Use | Claude 3.5 Sonnet v2 with computer use capabilities |
| May 2025 | Claude 4 Family | Opus 4 and Sonnet 4 with Extended Thinking |
| November 2025 | Claude Opus 4.5 | First model above 80% on SWE-bench Verified |
| February 2026 | Claude 4.6 | Opus 4.6 #1 on LMSYS High-Elo Bracket |

In less than three years, Anthropic has evolved from a small research lab to one of the most valuable startups in the world. Pretty impressive, considering the company was founded by just seven people.

## 10\. Constitutional AI and Safety

This is where Anthropic differs most clearly from the competition. While OpenAI and Google primarily focus on speed and new features, AI safety sits at the very top of Anthropic's agenda.

| Year | Initiative | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2022 | Constitutional AI | Training based on ethical principles instead of solely human feedback |
| 2023 | Responsible Scaling Policy 1.0 | Commitment: No models trained without guaranteed safety measures |
| 2024 | Model Spec & USAISI | Partnership with the US AI Safety Institute |
| May 2025 | ASL-3 Safeguards | Protection against chemical and biological threats implemented |
| Feb 2026 | RSP 3.0 | Updated safety policy with Frontier Safety Roadmaps |

Constitutional AI is Anthropic's core approach. Instead of relying solely on human feedback (RLHF), Claude is trained based on a set of ethical principles. The model learns to evaluate and improve its own responses based on this "constitution."

Anthropic's safety work has also influenced legislation: California's SB 53, New York's RAISE Act and the EU AI Act (Codes of Practice) now require frontier AI developers to publish risk frameworks.

## 13\. Facts and Records

And because statistics articles are simply better with a few entertaining facts:

01. **Namesake:** Claude is named after Claude Shannon, the "father of information theory." Shannon's work "A Mathematical Theory of Communication" (1948) has been cited over 160,000 times.
02. **First Non-OpenAI Model #1:** In March 2024, Claude 3 Opus became the first model from another provider to overtake GPT-4 on the LMSYS Chatbot Arena Leaderboard.
03. **Sibling Founders:** Anthropic was founded by siblings Dario and Daniela Amodei. Both previously held VP positions at OpenAI.
04. **Fastest Revenue Growth:** From $1 billion to $19 billion ARR in just 15 months.
05. **SWE-bench Record:** Claude Opus 4.5 was the first AI model to break the 80% mark on SWE-bench Verified (80.9%) in November 2025.
06. **Second-Largest VC Round:** The Series G at $30 billion in February 2026 was the second-largest venture capital round of all time.
07. **Claude Code Overtaking:** Claude Code launched in February 2025 and overtook GitHub Copilot and Cursor within 8 months as the most-used AI coding tool.
08. **Deloitte Record:** The deployment of Claude for 470,000+ Deloitte employees is the largest enterprise AI deployment by a single AI provider.
09. **Multi-Cloud Strategy:** Anthropic is the only major AI company simultaneously using Google TPUs, Amazon Trainium chips and NVIDIA GPUs.
10. **Shannon as Investor:** By the way, Claude Shannon was also a successful investor. He achieved an average return of about 28% per year from 1958 to 1986 (Warren Buffett: 27% from 1965 to 1995).
